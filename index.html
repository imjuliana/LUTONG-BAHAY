About Lutong Ulam

Lutong Ulam brings the authentic taste of Filipino home cooking to your table. Founded with a passion for traditional recipes and quality ingredients, we're committed to delivering meals that taste just like mom used to make.

Our Mission

To bring the authentic taste of Filipino home cooking to every household by providing delicious, nutritious, and affordable meals prepared with the highest quality ingredients and traditional cooking methods.

Our Vision

To become the most trusted and beloved Filipino food brand, recognized for our commitment to culinary excellence, customer satisfaction, and preserving the rich culinary heritage of the Philippines.
